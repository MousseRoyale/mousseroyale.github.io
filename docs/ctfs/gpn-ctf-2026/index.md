## Overview

Played this one solo and only had time for one of the two days, but it was really good. The whole event ran on a dining-room theme — the challenges and the dining-room framing were a lot of fun.

Worth knowing: scoring is dynamic, so a challenge's value decays as more teams solve it — retroactively, even for solves you'd already locked in. My usual strategy of targeting the highest-value challenges was a bit less efficient here as a result. I was second blood on **Culinary Circles** when it was the maximum 500 points; by the end of the event it had decayed to 119.
